My favourite city

My favourite city is Toronto in Canada. Toronto is one of the largest and most popular cities in Canada. It is known for its beautiful skyline, modern buildings, and diverse culture. People from many different countries live in Toronto, making it a multicultural city. The city is also home to famous landmarks like the CN Tower. I like Toronto because it is clean, organized, and offers many opportunities in technology and education. I would love to visit or live there someday.
